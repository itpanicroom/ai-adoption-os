# Skill: Executive Alignment

> **Status:** Placeholder — not implemented in v0.1

## Purpose

The Executive Alignment skill structures the business case for AI adoption and builds the executive narrative needed to secure sponsorship, funding, and sustained leadership engagement throughout the adoption lifecycle.

This skill ensures that AI adoption is positioned as a strategic business initiative — not a technology project.

## CAF Phase

**Strategy**

## Invoked By

- Strategy Agent

## Inputs

- Organizational input profile (company size, industry, executive sponsor status)
- Business objectives (if provided)
- Target AI technology (from Scenario Pack)

## Expected Outputs

- AI adoption vision statement tailored to organizational context
- Business case summary aligned to organizational priorities
- Executive sponsorship activation plan
- Key messages for leadership communication
- ROI framing for the specific AI technology being deployed

## Key Activities

1. Identify the primary business drivers for AI adoption in this organization
2. Frame AI value in business terms (productivity, revenue, risk reduction, competitive advantage)
3. Define the executive sponsor role and expected engagement activities
4. Identify potential executive objections and prepare responses
5. Structure the business case narrative for leadership presentation

## Notes

Executive alignment is not a one-time activity. This skill should be revisited at each major phase transition to maintain leadership engagement as adoption progresses.
